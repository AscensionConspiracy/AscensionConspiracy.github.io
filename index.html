Ascension Conspiracy

Ascension Conspiracy is the musical and performance vehicle of artist Greg Cedarbeard whose voice, or voices and compositions form the center of our musical expressions. We interweave songs in Rock, Pop, Shoegaze, Prog, Jazz with sequences in ~

Ambient, Drone, Soundscape, including visualizations and breathing exercises to guide participants in our manifestations into altered states of ecstatic bliss, off world exploration, aural healing;

in this way Ascension Conspiracy is a starship, not of metal, glass and plastic, but a starship of consciousness. This is old school tech of rhythm, meter, voice in a bardic way that brings forth vivid experiences in imaginal travel with only the waves of our sounds and the focus of our minds. In this way Ascension Conspiracy formulates one way to manifest Mer Ka Ba. On the other hand, it's just really good music.
